Descrição

“炎戈竜”。硬い嘴と強靭な筋力を使って岩盤を突き破り、地中から地上、さらには天井までをも縦横無尽に行き交う。全身にまとう硬化した溶岩は非常に硬いが、地中に潜った際の熱や、火属性、爆発による攻撃などで赤く変化し、柔らかくなるようだ.

Also known as "Fire Pike Wyvern". It uses its hard beak and strong muscles to break through rock layers, moving freely underground, on the ground, and even to the ceiling. The hardened lava that envelops its body is extremely tough, but when it burrows, the heat, fire attribute attacks, and explosions cause it to turn red and soften.
